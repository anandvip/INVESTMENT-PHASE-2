02 • Project Background & Sustainability Vision

Located in Pehowa (major banana mandi cluster), the unit sources 100% of raw bananas from mandis. Philosophy: complete utilisation of fruit, peel, pseudo-stem and residual biomass. No waste to landfill. Designed as a plug-and-play cash-flow engine for an agriculture-based startup that is still deciding its final business model.

03 • Product & Process Cascade (Zero-Waste)

STREAM 1 — FRUIT CORE

Green Banana Flour & Ripe Powder

Yield per 100 kg raw: 18–22 kg

Selling: ₹180–280/kg • Buyers: bakeries, gluten-free brands

STREAM 2 — PEEL

Peel Polyphenols, Pectin & Flour

Yield per 100 kg raw: 8–12 kg flour + 2–4 kg extract

FSSAI Health Supplement Regs 2022 (Schedule II/IV) required

STREAM 3 — FIBER

Pseudo-stem & Peel Fiber Bales

Yield per 100 kg raw: 1.5–2.5 kg dry bales

Selling: ₹80–150/kg • Non-food (BIS/GST only)

STREAM 4 — RESIDUAL

Vermicompost (FCO 1985)

Yield per 100 kg raw: 35–45 kg certified organic fertiliser

Selling: ₹12–18/kg
