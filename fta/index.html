**Summary:** ASEAN (Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam) – Goods 2010 / Services 2015 +
Established regional agreement providing tariff reductions and services liberalisation across Southeast Asia’s dynamic markets.

Key Trade Categories

  • Agriculture & food products (palm oil, spices, processed foods)
  • Electronics, machinery & auto components
  • Textiles, pharmaceuticals, and IT-enabled services

Best Use Cases for Entrepreneurs

**Summary:** Australia – ECTA 2022 +
